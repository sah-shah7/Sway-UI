Cards

Cards is a very flexible content contaniner. It can be made of various headers,paragraphs,colors etc. The cards in Sway UI are differentiate based on their layouts. By default all cards have box-shadow enabled. To disabled it, add "no-shadow" to class of card container. Pre-defined card types are :- Vertical, Horizontal, Text Only cards.
